Tomato Bacon Grilled Cheese Recipe

Here's how to make a grilled cheese sandwich, infused with bacon and tomato!

  • Ingredients

  • 8 slices of bacon
  • 1/4 cups of softened butter
  • 8 slices of white bread
  • 8 slices of American cheese
  • 8 slices of tomato

Instructions

Step 1

Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown.
Drain, and set aside.

Step 2

Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Spread butter onto one side of each slice of bread.
Lay 4 slices of bread, butter side down, in the skillet.
Top with a slice of cheese, 2 slice tomato, bacon, and another slice of cheese.
Cover with a slice a bread, butter side out. Fry sandwiches until golden on both sides.
